About Kaliente Nepal in Pokhara

Who are we?
A home in Pokhara
On the quiteter North Lakeside

Be our guest

Located in Pokhara, Kaliente pokhara is 1.7 km from Fewa Lake and features facilities like garden and bar. The property is close to several well-known attractions, 2.6 km from World Peace Pagoda, 4.1 km from International Mountain Museum and 6 km from Mahendra Cave. Guests can enjoy lake views. At the guest house rooms are equipped with a terrace. Kaliente pokhara provides some rooms with garden views, and each room is equipped with a balcony. The rooms will provide guests with a microwave. A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property. The accommodation offers a children's playground. Staff at the 24-hour front desk can provide information on what to do in the area. Begnas Lake is 14 km from Kaliente pokhara, while Mahendra Cave is 6 km away. This is our guests' favourite part of Pokhara, according to independent reviews. We speak your language!
